The film festival

“Watch it outside” is organizing an outdoor film festival and are set to launch this year an event : “Watch it outside Boston edition” to be held from August 5th to 8th in Boston Public Garden , a park in Boston that can hold up to 1,500 people.This event is not only aimed at residents of Boston and its surroundings, families and film lovers but also tourists.

We offer you, each evening, to discover or rediscover a selection of films. You can find the programs during the four days of the festival on our programs section. All film screenings start at 6 p.m. and last until midnight.

The event is free but, only registered people can attend the event, so hurry to register online to get a ticket and attend the film festival.
